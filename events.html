Recent Events:

  • Talk on "Cooperative Path Following of Robotic Vehicles using Event based Control and Communication" with Prof. Karl Henrik Johansson's research group, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden on October 16, 2017.

  • Participated in summer school on "A Systems and Control Perspective on Privacy, Safety, and Security in large-scale Cyber-Physical Systems" organized by Dutch Institute of Systems and Control on July 3 - July 6, 2017 at The Hague, Netherlands.

  • Participated in the First MarineUAS summer school organized by Instituto Superior Técnico, Lisbon, Portugal from June 19 - June 29, 2017.
